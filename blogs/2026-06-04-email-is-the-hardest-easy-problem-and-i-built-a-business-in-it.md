The founder of EmailEngine reflects on building a sustainable business around email infrastructure, growing from 6,100 euros monthly recurring revenue to approximately $16,000/month serving 204 customers. Success came not from clever code but from consistently handling unglamorous work: managing quirks across countless email providers, debugging connection issues, and maintaining reliability systems that customers never see working.
